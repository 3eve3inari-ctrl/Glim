## Template Example — Glim Campaign

### 1. Understanding the Change
**What it means:** Gathering significant evidence about how transformation works—confirming theories, discovering patterns, learning hidden truths.
**Examples:**
- Confirming the acceptance/resistance theory with Sable's data
- Learning the seals contain living prisoners
- Receiving the knowledge of unmaking from the Pale Court

### 2. Resolving a Changed Situation
**What it means:** Meaningful resolutions with other Changed—combat, talking one down, guiding a transformation, mercy-killing one too far gone.
**Examples:**
- Helping Sanna through her first transformation
- Capturing Silk alive instead of killing him
- The entity negotiation at Thornhallow

### 3. Maintaining Humanity
**What it means:** Choices that keep the character tethered to who they were. Compassion, restraint, connection, vulnerability.
**Examples:**
- Listening to Aldric Bryce's grief instead of burning him
- Breaking down and accepting support from allies
- Fleeing the battlefield rather than pursuing in rage

### 4. Mastering the Form
**What it means:** Learning what the new body/abilities can do without losing yourself. Control, precision, new capabilities.
**Examples:**
- Achieving fire breath precision (spiral control, variable intensity)
- First successful battlefield smoke screen
- Learning Gentle Precision at Apex scale

### 5. Political/Social Victories
**What it means:** Rebuilding influence, turning enemies to allies, protecting position, changing minds.
**Examples:**
- Convincing the King to support the manufactured faith
- Negotiating the truce with Corin
- The Convocation revelation surviving intact

**Example — Dragon Size (Glim):**

### Size Tiers
| Size | Description | Mechanical Effects |
|------|-------------|-------------------|
| House-sized (~40 ft) | Starting | Baseline |
| Manor-sized (~60 ft) | First growth | +1 Physical, Intimidation +1 tier |
| Keep-sized (~100 ft) | Regional presence | +1 Physical, hiding impossible |
| Fortress-sized (~150 ft) | Landmark | +1 Physical, affects geography |
| Apex (~200 ft) | Ancient dragon scale | Maximum; Physical capped |

### Growth Triggers
Size increases occur during:
- Moments of profound acceptance of dragon nature
- Time skips after significant prophetic acts
- Major story milestones
- Fully embracing draconic instincts without apology

### Growth Philosophy
Growth is *discovered, not witnessed*—happens between scenes. The player roleplays the reaction and discovery, not the transformation itself.
