### Chapter 1, Session 3 — "Halric Did Not Return"

Toren spent the day in apprenticeship with Kenric. Kenric stopped mid-sketch, stared at his density grid, and said the brightness is *not* centered on the bear-stone — it's offset east, and he doesn't yet know why. He asked her to come to the workshop tonight after the captain dismissed her. By sunset, Halric had not returned. The captain's order: no rescue tonight. Toren leads the morning party.

> **Mechanics shown:**
> - "Kenric's mapping" clock ticked 1 → 3/8 (research breakthrough seed planted; long-arc clock surfaced)
> - New tactical clock created: "Halric overdue" 1/4 at sundown, ticked to 2/4 by second watch
> - "Bear-line patience" ticked 1 → 4/6 (Hold dispatched another scout into the same range without contact attempt)

### Chapter 1, Session 2 — "The Eastern Ridge"

Toren and Halric rode east at dawn to look for sign of Bren. Found a deer carcass arranged wrong — bones placed deliberately, no scavenger marks. Later, a deer with an extra crown of antlers crossed their path and was gone when they looked twice. Returned by sundown without finding Bren but with the certain feeling of having been *watched the whole way*.

> **Mechanics shown:**
> - Oracle "Is the deer Glim-touched?" set to **likely** (3-6) — Die 1: 5 (yes), Die 2: 6 (and), Die 3: 4 (neutral) → **Yes, and...** (and Toren's notebook now has a sketch she does not show Kenric)
> - Oracle "Are we unobserved?" set to **unlikely** (5-6) — Die 1: 2 (no), Die 2: 4 (plain), Die 3: 1 (bane) → bane promoted plain → and → **No, and...** (the observers were *focused*; this isn't passing wildlife attention)
> - "Bear-line patience" ticked 1 → 3/6 because the bane locked in the worst-case observation

### Chapter 1, Session 1 — "Replacement"

Toren arrived at Lantern Hold to fill the slot left by Bren. Captain Daven interviewed her cool and short, then took her on a wall-walk and asked what she'd change about the watch rotation. Toren named two flaws and was given ranging permission. Met Kenric, who agreed to teach her Glim sensing twice a week. Met Sarith at the well, who showed her the loose floorboard.

> **Mechanics shown:**
> - Oracle "Is Captain Daven receptive to the Glim apprenticeship?" set to **fifty-fifty** — Die 1: 4 (yes), Die 2: 3 (plain), Die 3: 10 (boon) → boon promoted plain → and → **Yes, and...** (the captain not only permitted it but offered Kenric extra rations to take Toren on properly)
> - "Captain's assessment" clock created at 6 segments and resolved within the session — see `clocks.json` `completed` for the full step record and resolution narrative
