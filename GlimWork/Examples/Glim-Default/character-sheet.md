# Toren Vesh

## Identity

**Name:** Toren Vesh
**Concept:** Journeyman scout on the Lantern Hold charter — young, capable in the woods, learning to see the Glim from Kenric Maul

**Description:**
Twenty-two, lean, brown-haired, brown-eyed. Tan from a season outside. Wears charter-issue gray wool, a hooded cloak in field-green, and a cracked leather quiver. Carries a yew bow she made herself the spring before signing on, a long knife, and a thin leather notebook she fills with observations she does not show others.

**Background:**
Eldest of three from a hill-village a week's ride from the homeland coast. Father gone before she could walk; mother holds the family together by selling carded wool. Toren signed the charter for the wages — half goes home each season — and because the woods had stopped feeling large enough. Has been at Lantern Hold roughly four months, joined as a replacement scout after the spring losses.
